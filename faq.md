## General Questions

### What is the Maison Craft theme?

Maison is a minimalist, design-focused Shopify theme built for artisan brands, curated collections, and craft-driven businesses. It features 30+ customizable sections, 4 style presets, and a focus on storytelling through beautiful imagery and typography.

### What Shopify plan do I need?

Maison works with all Shopify plans:
- ✅ Shopify Basic
- ✅ Shopify
- ✅ Advanced Shopify
- ✅ Shopify Plus

Some features (like multi-currency) require specific plans or payment gateways.

### Is the theme mobile-responsive?

Yes! Maison is fully responsive and mobile-first. It automatically adapts to all screen sizes:
- Mobile phones (320px+)
- Tablets (768px+)
- Desktops (1024px+)
- Large screens (1440px+)

### Can I use this theme for any type of store?

While Maison is designed for artisan/craft businesses, it works for any store selling:
- ✅ Home goods & decor
- ✅ Handmade products
- ✅ Fashion & accessories
- ✅ Art & prints
- ✅ Furniture
- ✅ Beauty & wellness
- ✅ Jewelry
- ✅ Any curated collection

### How many products can I add?

No limit! Maison can handle stores with:
- Small catalogs (10-50 products)
- Medium catalogs (50-500 products)
- Large catalogs (500+ products)

Performance remains excellent with proper image optimization.

### Does the theme support multiple languages?

Yes! Maison supports Shopify's multi-language features:
- Manual translation of theme content
- Works with translation apps (Langify, Weglot)
- RTL (right-to-left) language support
- Multi-currency display

### Can I sell internationally?

Absolutely! Maison includes:
- Multi-currency support
- International shipping configuration
- Tax calculation for multiple countries
- Localized checkout experience

See our [Checkout Settings Guide](CHECKOUT_SETTINGS.md) for setup instructions.
