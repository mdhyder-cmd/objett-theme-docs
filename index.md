A minimalist, design-focused Shopify theme for artisan brands, curated collections, and craft-driven businesses.

## Features

### Design & Aesthetics
✨ **4 Beautiful Style Presets** - Linen, Noir, Sage, and Atelier  
🎨 **Fully Customizable** - Colors, fonts, and layouts  
📱 **Mobile Responsive** - Perfect on all devices  
🖼️ **High-Quality Imagery** - Full-width hero sections

### E-commerce Features
🛒 **Smart Cart Drawer** - Seamless shopping experience  
🔍 **Advanced Filtering** - Help customers find products  
❤️ **Wishlist Support** - Let customers save favorites  
⭐ **Product Recommendations** - Increase sales

### Performance & Technical
⚡ **Fast Load Times** - Optimized for performance  
♿ **Accessibility Compliant** - WCAG guidelines  
🔍 **SEO Optimized** - Structured data and meta tags  
🌐 **Multi-Currency Ready** - Global commerce support
